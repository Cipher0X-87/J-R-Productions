ABOUT US:

We are a small group of writers, artists and computer experts looking to make these stories our own for your enjoyment. Our stories are mostly based off mythologies, spirituality and the unknown/occult. As these topics are beyond fascinating in our eyes.

How do we produce these stories?

They normally start out in the form of a roleplay, a dungeon master and players all playing together through text based posts. This is not like DND. There is a post order that we follow, each writer playing as a character or many characters. The dungeon master is the one that sets up The enviornment and guides the narative of the story/book/chapter. So this allows a lot of freedom in the story.

When the story is done it is modified for a reader, everything is reviewed for consistency and reading fluidity.
